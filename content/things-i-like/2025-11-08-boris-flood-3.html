--- title: boris - flood 3 ---

boris - flood 3

i love boris.

when this track descends into sludgey perdition, they play a cool rhythmic trick. the guitar phrase is 14 beats long, but the drums play a 16 beat phrase and they cycle around, out of phase, until they unite again in unison.

if you listen to the studio version with headphones, there's also a full stereo pan of the squealing lead guitar. ugggh i love it.
