Monstergarden’s Worldbuilding

Monstergarden is a project by a concept artist enhandled Monlez

He’s writing and illustrating a world of reinterpreted fantasy tropes, one vlog at a time. The project is part process, part showcase, part loretelling.

He also crowdsources ideas during livestreams, an egregore of imagination, the most recent of which resulted in his take on gnomes: curiously intelligent vegetables that leave the loam, grow limbs, and roam.

His style’s macabre, verging on gross. It reminds me of The Goblin Companion by Brian Froud and Terry Jones.
